# Georgia Rules (Akoma Ntoso XML)

Georgia tax and benefit statutes and regulations encoded in Akoma Ntoso XML format.

## Structure

```
rules-us-ga/
├── statutes/
│   ├── title-48/     # Revenue and Taxation
│   └── title-49/     # Social Services
└── regulations/      # Rules and Regulations of Georgia
```

## Sources

### Statutes
- **Official Code of Georgia Annotated (O.C.G.A.)**
  - Title 48: Revenue and Taxation
  - Title 49: Social Services, Public Assistance, and Human Resources

### Regulations
- **Rules and Regulations of Georgia (Ga. Comp. R. & Regs.)**
  - Department of Revenue regulations
  - Department of Human Services regulations

## Format

All files use [Akoma Ntoso](http://www.akomantoso.org/) XML format, the international standard for legal documents.
